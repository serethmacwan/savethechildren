The foundation


'By the same way Father in heaven is not willing that any of these little ones should be list' Mathew 18:14
Save the Children trust was founded by seeing children begging on the streets of Fatehgunj, Vadodara and with a very simple vision of bringing children near to God and not letting them drift away.

The founder late. Mr Philip Christian believed that children being the future, if nurtured in tender age can become faithful and God fearing adults. He knew that youth of the Church has the power to become fruitful offspring if guided in the right path.

As Save the children completes its 30 years of ministry, we started from Bible study for children to conducting crusades. We thank all the donors, students, family members and Churches that have helped and trusted us.

Vision

 To conduct programs for physical, mental and spiritual development of children without any discrimination of caste, creed, and race.  To cater the educational problems of children both financially and technically. To organise Interactive Educational Programmes led by experts of the respective subjects to establish children's interest in subjects and providing necessary requisites for the same. To provide guidance and motivation to the children and encourage them to pursue higher education by keeping their academic interests elevated.  To integrate values and ethics in the children right from their childhood ensuring that they become responsible and worthy citizens with a view to serve and sacrifice for this great country. Alongside, to ensure that none of the children go wayward in direction of activities that are harmful for the society or the country's pride and honour.  To visit juvenile criminals, reformative centers and remand homes to understand, empathise with their problems and improving their moral standards. To motivate such misguided children on a continuous basis and influence them to seek knowledge and education and turn them into country's finest citizens by conducting necessary encouraging programs in the direction.  All the above mentioned activities must be conducted by the Trust, keeping God's fear and ubiquity in mind.  For the poor, suppressed, backwards and disabled as well as for the orphan kids' educational requirements, provision of necessary means of earning must be looked into.  To focus on children following Christianity, the Trust shall put efforts to bring them closer to Jesus Christ through a special program and shape their spiritual life as well. This program shall allow the children of other castes to equally participate with prior permission and willingness of their parents.  This Trust shall conduct various child-friendly programs aimed at the children's rights as well as their physical, mental and spiritual well-being and growth.

Mission

The motto of this Trust shall be "The Father in the skies never wishes for the destruction of anyone amongst his children".
